{% trans "How can I configure my devices?" %}

{% trans "How can I add a new device?" %}

{% trans "Open the "gPodder Podcast-Client" on your PC or MAC." %}

{% trans "Upload your podcasts and if it's asked, enter your username and password. The server will create a new legacy device for you, if no device exists." %}

{% trans "How can I edit a device?" %}

{% trans "Click on your "Subscriptions"- or "Devices"-link." %}
{% trans "Now you can see your devices, click on one of them." %}

{% trans "You are redirected to another page.
On the bottom of the page there's a field named "Edit"" %}

{% trans "Here you can edit the name, the type and the uid. But pay attention if you edit the uid! If you enter a false one your subscriptions won't be synchronize with your device anymore." %}

{% trans "How can I configure the synchronisation between devices?" %}

{% trans "How can I start a synchronisation?" %}

{% trans "Choose a device on your "Subscriptions"- or "Device"-screen and click on it." %}

{% trans "Go to the field "Synchronize", choose your desired device and click on the "OK"-button." %}

{% trans "Now the two devices are synchronized. If this isn't enough, you can synchronize your device with one or more devices!" %}

{% trans "How can I stop a synchronisation?" %}

{% trans "Click on your "Subscriptions"- or "Devices"-link." %}
{% trans "Choose a device on which you would like to stop the synchronisation and click on it." %}

{% trans "Go to the field "Synchronize" and click on "Stop synchronisation for your device"" %}

{% trans "Now the two devices are unsynchronized again." %}

{% trans "How can I copy single subscriptions between devices?" %}

{% trans "Click on your "Subscriptions"-link." %}
{% trans "Choose the podcast which you would like to copy on another device and click on it." %}

{% trans "Below the field "Devices" you see the link "Subscribe to ... on other devices" click on it." %}

{% trans "On the next page you can choose your desired device on which you want to copy the subscription." %}
{% trans "After hitting the "OK"-button, the subscription has been copied." %}

{% trans "How can I delete subscriptions from a device?" %}

{% trans "There are different methods to do that:" %}

  • {% trans "You can simply delete the subscription on your client and upload your new subscription-list" %}
  • {% trans "or you can choose the podcast which you'd like to unsubscribe and click on it." %}

    {% trans "Below the field "Devices" you see on which devices you've subscribed the podcast. Now select the device on which the podcast schould be unsubscribed and click on the red/orange-minus on the right." %}

    {% trans "You are redirected to the same page, now the device from which you unsubscribed the podcast shouldn't exist amongst "Devices" anymore." %}
